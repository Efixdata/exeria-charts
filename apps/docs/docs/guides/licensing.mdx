# Licensing

Exeria Charts is source-available under the Exeria Charts Source Available License 1.0.

## The practical summary

- personal use is allowed
- educational use is allowed
- qualifying open source projects are allowed
- qualifying small-scale use is allowed under the Additional Use Grant
- broader commercial use requires a separate commercial license from Efix Data Sp. z o. o.

## Before you ship

Review the full legal terms in the repository:

- `LICENSE`
- `LICENSING.md`

If your use case is commercial, revenue-generating, or customer-facing at scale, do not treat the Additional Use Grant as a substitute for a commercial agreement.
